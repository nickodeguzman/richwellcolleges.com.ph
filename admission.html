Admission

General Guidelines

Every student-applicant should accomplish the following guidelines:

  1. An applicant must complete the application procedure to be considered an official applicant.
  2. Student-applicant must fill up the student’s application form and submit in the Admission’s Office.
  3. College applicants must choose one college or diploma program. Senior High School applicants must choose one track and one strand.
  4. After accomplishing the pre-registration procedure, the student-applicant will be scheduled for an entry assessment in the form of a written and one-on-one interview.
  5. Student-applicant must submit the needed documents as enrollment requirements.

Requirements

Freshman:

  • Application form including 2x2 ID photos
  • Original form 138 (High School Report Card)
  • Original Certificate of Good Moral Character (signed by the Principal)
  • Photocopy of Authenticated Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • NCAE result
  • Marriage Contract (if married)
  • Long brown envelope

Transferee:

  • Application form including 2x2 ID photos
  • Transcript of Records
  • Original Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Honorable Dismissal or Transfer Credential
  • Certificate of Eligibility to Transfer (if Honorable Dismissal/Transfer Credential is not yet available)
  • Photocopy of PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Marriage Contract (if married)
  • Long brown envelope
